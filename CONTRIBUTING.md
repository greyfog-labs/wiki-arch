# Contributing

greyfog-labs projects are **personal research artifacts** published openly. They may not be actively maintained or looking for external contributions.

## What's welcome

- **Bug reports** — if something breaks, open an issue
- **Questions** — if the docs are unclear, ask
- **Forks** — MIT license, take it anywhere

## What's not expected

- Pull requests may not be reviewed in a timely manner (or at all)
- Feature requests may be ignored if they don't align with internal direction
- This is not a community project — it's shared research

## Tone

If you open an issue or discussion, keep it civil. This is a fog — not a spotlight.

**greyfog-labs** — MIT. Take what works. Leave what doesn't. Vanish.
